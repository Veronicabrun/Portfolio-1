About Me

Hello, my name is Veronica. I'm 34 years old, have two children, and live with my partner just outside Drammen. I have a vocational diploma in sales and have worked in retail for 16 years. Throughout these years, I've held various roles, from sales assistant to visual merchandiser and assistant store manager. Additionally, I handle some inventory orders and hold a forklift operator's license.

About 2.5 years ago, I decided to make a career change, and I chose to become a front-end developer. I felt that there were many opportunities within front-end development, as it encompasses both UI/UX design and interaction design, which I find very exciting, along with the technical aspect. I decided to pursue my studies part-time online alongside my current full-time job, as it suited my life situation best.

Through my portfolio, I'll be sharing projects from my studies as well as personal hobby projects. Currently, I'm actively seeking relevant job opportunities in my field, whether full-time, part-time, or as an intern/trainee. Feel free to get in touch for a chat!
